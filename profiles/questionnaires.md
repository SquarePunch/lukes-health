# Profile Questionnaires

This document contains lists of questions to help you continuously build and refine your psychological, medical, and nutrition profiles. Whenever you have the energy, you can answer these questions and integrate the insights into the respective profile files.

## Psychological Profile Questions

1. **Values & Motivations:**
   - What are your core values in life?
   - What activities make you lose track of time (in a positive, fulfilling way)?
2. **Stress & Triggers:**
   - Aside from time pressure and public speaking, are there specific types of social interactions or situations that reliably trigger your anxiety?
   - What physical sensations do you notice in your body right before a panic attack or depressive drop begins?
3. **Coping Mechanisms:**
   - What are some healthy, substance-free ways you've successfully managed stress in the past, even if only briefly?
   - When you feel the urge to people-please, what thoughts usually run through your head?
4. **Goals & Future Self:**
   - What does a "good day" look like for your mental health when you are completely sober?
   - What boundaries do you feel you need to set with others to protect your energy?

## Medical Profile Questions

1. **Family History:**
   - Are there any recurring medical conditions (physical or mental) in your immediate or extended family?
2. **Pain Management:**
   - What specific movements or postures seem to aggravate your herniated disc?
   - What movements or postures alleviate it?
   - How soon after noticing the shoulder/ulnaris nerve pain do you typically seek massage therapy? Could you schedule it proactively?
3. **Preventative Care:**
   - When was your last comprehensive blood panel or physical exam? Were there any notable deficiencies (e.g., Vitamin D, Iron, B12)?
   - Are you currently seeing a therapist, or is that something you are open to exploring for long-term support?

## Nutrition Profile Questions

1. **Baseline Diet (When Healthy):**
   - When your appetite is normal, what are your favorite, easy-to-make meals?
   - Are there any specific foods that reliably upset your stomach or cause brain fog?
2. **Hydration & Caffeine:**
   - Roughly how much plain water do you drink per day, excluding coffee and Club Mate?
   - Do you notice a correlation between high caffeine/sugar intake (like the Club Mate) and the intensity of your morning anxiety or insomnia?
3. **Macronutrients:**
   - Do you consciously track your protein intake, especially now that you are taking creatine and trying to maintain your energy?
