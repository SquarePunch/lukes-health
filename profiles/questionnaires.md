# Profile Questionnaires

This document contains lists of questions to help you continuously build and refine your psychological, medical, and nutrition profiles. Whenever you have the energy, you can answer these questions and integrate the insights into the respective profile files.

## Psychological Profile Questions

1. **Flow Arts & Routine:**
   - How often do you currently practice flowarts/poi spinning, and does it sustainably improve your mood over time or just in the moment?
   - What prevents you from turning this into a daily or highly regular habit?
2. **Community & Psychedelics:**
   - Could you elaborate on how your therapeutic community psychedelic experiences affect your mood over the following weeks?
   - Are there specific integration practices your community uses after these sessions?
3. **Boundaries & Energy:**
   - You mentioned realizing you cannot do everything asked of you. Are there specific areas (family, friends, work) where you find it easiest to say "no", and areas where it is still difficult?

## Medical Profile Questions

1. **Cholesterol & Diet:**
   - Given the slightly high cholesterol and your paternal family history, would you be open to adjusting your diet (e.g., adding more fish, reducing the amount of scrambled eggs/meat salad)?
2. **Preventative Management:**
   - Besides stretching and avoiding long sitting, are there any ergonomic changes at your workspace you could make to protect your lower spine and ulnaris nerve?

## Nutrition Profile Questions

1. **Hydration Strategies:**
   - Do you have ideas on how to increase your daily plain water intake (currently ~0.5L), or do you feel it is currently unmanageable? Would keeping a water bottle by your desk help?
2. **Protein Tracking:**
   - Would you be open to roughly estimating your protein intake for just a few days to see if you are hitting a healthy baseline, especially since you are taking creatine?
