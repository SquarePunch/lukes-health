# Inherited Knowledge

This document stores distilled, long-term lessons and rules that have proven effective over time.

## Physical Health

- **Shoulder & Spine Maintenance:** Extreme shoulder pain due to a clenched ulnaris nerve was successfully resolved with massage therapy. If this pain reoccurs, prioritize massage therapy. Additionally, upper spine cracking requires specific mobility movements ("crack moves") to prevent the back from clogging up and causing arm tingling ("kribbeln").
- **Substance Cessation:** Tapering off Kratom/Cannabis is historically unmanageable; cessation requires going cold turkey. Moderation is difficult on its own, so leveraging structural barriers (such as a regular job that reduces alone time, knowing that use is strongly linked to being alone and masturbation) is an effective strategy. Stopping abruptly after prolonged use triggers rapid mood drops, depressive episodes, and severe fatigue/insomnia within weeks. Caution and routines are vital during this period.
- **Morning Anxiety Management:** Getting up earlier (e.g., 10:00 instead of noon) and exposing oneself to early morning light (e.g., opening windows by 8:00) has proven effective in reducing morning anxiety.
- **Caffeine & Insomnia:** Consuming caffeine (Mate/coffee) late in the evening (18:00 - 19:00), especially when combined with attempts to force exercise, can override SSRI sedation and cause severe insomnia (e.g., awake until 5 AM). A strong rule is to switch to caffeine-free alternatives after 16:00.
- **Digestion & Caffeine:** Consuming caffeine on an empty stomach exacerbates stomach distress. Eating solid food (e.g., eggs, cornflakes) prior to the first caffeinated drink helps manage gastrointestinal issues.
- **Stress & Employment:** Taking on high-pressure, time-constrained goals (like rushing self-employment to avoid a job) heavily triggers anxiety, "headless chicken" behavior, and depressive episodes. Stability is key for mental baseline.
- **Substance Interactions (Kratom/Weed + SSRIs):** Mixing Kratom or Cannabis with Escitalopram (Lexapro/SSRI) significantly increases the risk of Serotonin Syndrome, which can be dangerous. It can also cause unexpected metabolic interactions or increase baseline anxiety (especially THC). Strict rule: Do not mix these.
- **Managing PAWS (Post-Acute Withdrawal Syndrome):** After acute withdrawal from Kratom/Weed, psychological symptoms (depression, anxiety, low motivation, cravings) can persist for weeks or months. Recognize these as PAWS rather than a permanent baseline shift.
- **Restoring Balance:** During and after withdrawal, establish strict routines to naturally restore dopamine and serotonin levels. This includes daily exercise, consistent sleep schedules, staying hydrated, and avoiding other substances that spike neurotransmitters artificially. Prioritize foundational pillars like diet and exercise over adding new supplements for energy recovery.
