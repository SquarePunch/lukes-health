# Inherited Knowledge

This document stores distilled, long-term lessons and rules that have proven effective over time.

## Physical Health
- **Shoulder Pain (Ulnaris Nerve):** Extreme shoulder pain due to a clenched ulnaris nerve was successfully resolved with massage therapy. If this pain reoccurs (as it seems to annually), prioritize scheduling massage therapy immediately.
- **Substance Cessation:** Stopping Kratom/Cannabis abruptly after prolonged use has historically triggered rapid mood drops and depressive episodes within weeks. Caution and possibly tapering/medical support should be considered for any future cessation attempts.
- **Stress & Employment:** Taking on high-pressure, time-constrained goals (like rushing self-employment to avoid a job) heavily triggers anxiety, "headless chicken" behavior, and depressive episodes. Stability is key for mental baseline.
